Our Services

Essential Infrastructure Monitoring

24/7 monitoring of Windows and Linux servers including CPU, memory, disk, and uptime to ensure availability.

Network & Device Monitoring

Monitoring of firewalls, switches, routers and network connectivity to ensure stable operations.

Application & Service Monitoring

Health checks for critical services such as web servers, databases, and directory services and more.

Alerting & Incident Awareness

Clear and actionable alerts delivered via email or messaging when issues occur.

Reporting & Operational Insights

Monthly reports covering uptime, performance trends, and capacity indicators.

How We Work

  • Assess your environment and monitoring requirements
  • Deploy and configure secure monitoring tools
  • Define thresholds aligned to business impact
  • Continuously monitor systems and notify stakeholders
  • Provide regular reports and recommendations

Client Onboarding

  • Initial discovery and scope definition
  • Secure access configuration (read-only where possible)
  • Deployment of monitoring checks
  • Alert testing and validation
  • Operational handover and reporting setup

Service Governance

Monitoring services are delivered under a simple service agreement defining scope, alerting methods, reporting frequency, and responsibilities. Incident resolution and remediation are excluded unless explicitly agreed.
